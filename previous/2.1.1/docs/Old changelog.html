What is Minicraft?

Minicraft is a game made by Notch himself within 48 hours. It was part of the of the 22nd Ludum Dare competition. The competition required that the developer make a game in 48 hours after hearing the theme of the contest. The theme was Alone, and the idea of killing the last living person, the Air Wizard, was created.

Minicraft in a nutshell is a 2-D Minecraft. You collect resources to make tools, by either mining them up or killing enemy mobs. go through the different layers to collect Iron, Gold, to eventually make a Gem Pickaxe which will allow you to brake the Hard Rock that is blocking you from entering the stairs up to the sky where the Air Wizard lies.

You can play Minicraft here: https://s3.amazonaws...ld22/index.html


K then, What is Minicraft+?

Minicraft+ is a mod of minicraft that makes minicraft more expansive and continuoes like Minecraft. I've been working on this mod since deciember 24th 2011. I also have some help from a friend of mine (Dillyg10) who has been working with java & many coding languages for years, so that is a bonus of its own. The Dungeon:
--------------



The dungeon is a new layer below the nether. It is home to two new mobs: Knights and Snakes.

The Knights drop shards in which they are used to upgrade your sword into a claymore.

The Snakes drop scales in which they are used to craft Scale armor (4 points).

Obsidian walls which block the entrance can only be destroyed with a Gold/Gem pickaxe and after you destroy the air wizard. You can also use TNT to blast through them as well, but your gonna need a lot to get through some walls in the dungeon.

The dungeon doesn't have bosses yet, that will be in 1.6 though!

Torches:
-------------------------



Torches are finally here! Torches allow you to light up blocks in the surface to prevent enemy mobs from spawning at night. Torches are crafted with 1 wood and 1 coal in player crafting.

Now you can light up your base so mobs won't spawn in it! But watch out, you can take out torches with your fists, so be careful where you swing!

Torches will only work on the surface as of this update.

The only blocks not affected by torches is tilled soil and wheat tile(because I was lazy), lava, and any walls/rocks.

Bows & Arrows:
---------------






Bows are a new tool that shoot arrows towards mobs to do damage. In survival mode you get 25 arrows, and in creative you get infinite to mess around with.

The bow is also a good tool to use against the air wizard when he is flying in the air.

Creative & Hardcore modes:
---------------------------------------

Hardcore - No respawning!

Creative - Basically Dillyg10's creative mode, but in Minicraft+. Has infinite placement of tiles, no hearts or hunger to worry about.


Beds:
--------------



Beds are a new furniture that allow you to skip the night to daytime.

Beds are crafted in the Loom from 3 wool & 5 planks.


Hunger System:
-------------------------





Food no longer heals you directly anymore! What do you do? Get those hamburgers up of course!

The bar heals you when it's full and hurts you when it's empty. On easy mode, it leaves 5 hearts left, on normal it leaves 3 hearts, and on Hard mode it leaves 0 (You die).

Apples, raw Porkchops and Beef give 1 point.

Bread gives 2 points

Cooked Porkchops and steak gives 3 points

Golden Apples fill all 10 points in the hunger meter


Armor System:
---------------------



Armor is a new system that is like "Extra HP". It works like this: You have 3 HP left, with 5 Armor points. Creeper blows up near you doing 7 Damage. Your armor points take up 5 points leaving you with 1 HP left and still alive.

There are 4 Types of armor: Leather, Iron, Gold, and Gem. Leather gives 3 points, Iron gives 5 points, gold gives 7 points, and gem gives you 10 points.

And don't think you can just put on 2 leather armors. If the armor bar has at least 1 armor point left you cannot put any other armor on.

Leather armor is made from 10 leather and is made in either the loom or a workbench.
Iron armor is made from 10 Iron and is made in the Anvil.
Gold armor is made from 10 Gold and is made in the Anvil.
Gem armor is made from 65 Gems and is made in the Anvil.


Animals:
---------------


Getting lonely in this 128 by 128 tile world? Well say hello to these fresh meats! I mean Animals.

Cows, Pigs, and sheep spawn on grass in the overworld. Peaceful animals that give food and resources that is required for beds, looms, and leather armor.

Pigs drop raw porkchops to eat. Cows drop raw beef to eat and leather to make leather armor. Sheep drop their wool to make all sorts of items. Raw Beef and Porkchops can be cooked in the Oven with 1 coal for each.

Loom:
----------


The loom is a new crafting station that is crafted by 10 wood and 5 wool. It is the station to craft the colored wool, beds, cloth, beds, and Leather armor.

Day/Night System
------------------------

Because every mod of Minicraft needs one! But unlike others, I don't use the Fog of war. I actually took my time and recolored all the terrain on the surface to make it look nice.

Day/night lasts 14 minutes and has 4 stages (Sunrise, Day, Sunset, Night).


Day lasts 7 minutes

Sunrise/Sunset lasts 1 minute

Night lasts 5 minutes.

You start at sunrise, so you have a total of 9 minutes of time before mobs start spawning at the surface.



Enemy mobs only come out at night on the surface, they spawn at all times at the sky/underground.

[Note: Since There is no way to light up the night at the moment, You can place down Stone Bricks to prevent them from spawning (Though they still can walk on it).]

Crafting:

Workbench - 10 Wood

Hatchet:

Wood - 3 Wood

Stone - 3 Wood, 3 Stone

The hatchet is a weaker version of the Axe, but is cheaper.

Pick:

Wood - 3 Wood

Stone - 3 Wood, 3 Stone

The pick is a weaker version of the Pickaxe that does not drop coal. It is only recommended to get stone from the very start of the game.

Spade:

Wood - 3 Wood

Stone - 3 Wood, 3 Stone

The spade is a weaker version of the shovel. It's pretty much the same but costs more stamina to use and does not drop seeds from grass as the shovel does.

Plank:

1 Wood

Plank is used for Wooden Housing, and can be placed on water and Holes. Can only be taken down with a Hatchet/Axe



Plank Wall:

3 planks

Plank walls are a part of Wooden Housing, Is used for blocking off enemies off and making your home look awesome. Can only be placed on Planks.



Plank Door:

5 planks

Plank Doors are a part of Wooden Housing, Hit it with your hands to open/shut it. Closed doors act like walls. Can only be placed on Planks. Can only be taken down with a Hatchet/Axe




Housing
------------

By Request I have added Floors, walls, and Doors. Wooden & Stone.

Wood Housing - Consists of Planks, Plank walls, and Plank Doors.

Planks are made from 1 wood in player crafting.
Plank walls are made from 3 planks in player crafting.
Plank Doors are made from 5 planks in player crafting.




Stone Housing - Consists of Stone Brick, Stone Brick Wall, and Stone Door.

Stone Brick:

Workbench - 2 Stone

Stone brick is the Flooring for Stone Housing, Prevents Mobs from spawning on the tile.



Stone Brick Wall:

Workbench - 3 Stone Brick

Stone Brick walls are used to block off enemies and make your home look Epic. Can only be placed on Stone Brick, and can only be removed by Pick or Pickaxe



Stone Brick Doors:

Workbench - 5 Stone Brick

Stone Brick Doors can be opened by hitting it with a empty hand, they act like a wall when closed. Can only be placed on Stone Brick. Can only be removed with pick or Pickaxe.
